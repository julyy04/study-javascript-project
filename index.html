GURUFIN PROJECT INTRODUCTION

GURUFIN PROJECT INTRODUCTION

The GURUFIN Foundation is spearheading the GURUFIN Project which focuses on building a new Layer-1 global financial infrastructure. We have developed the GURUFIN Layer-1 Mainnet for the past 4 years and completed a testnet in 2021. We have incorporated the Cyrex payment service provider (PSP) which has been operational in the real economy for the past 8 years. We also plan to link the Cyrexa digital bank to the GURUFIN infrastructure for easier and more convenient financial payment services for users.

To ensure a healthy GURU Ecosystem, the Foundation will only participate in projects that have been thoroughly reviewed, verified, and approved by experts and community in the respective fields. GURUFIN will provide a solid infrastructure and platform that merges real economy financial payment services with the Web3 world.

GURUFIN GOALS

The GURUFIN Ecosystem is expected to generate high transaction volume in the next 2 to 3 years and will also provide remittance and settlement services. It will operate through billions of dollars deposited in Cyrexa and its affiliate banks in major countries all over the world.

INFRASTRUCTURE

INFRASTRUCTURE

GURUFIN Layer 1 Mainnet

The GURUFIN Chain, which is a Cosmos-based blockchain with EVM integration, was developed with the GURUFIN SDK. Continuous R&D allowed for an upgraded and enhanced GURUFIN SDK to reduce the block generation time from 5 seconds to 3 seconds. This allows the core engine to reliably handle high transaction volumes at a faster rate.

Cyrexa (Digital Bank)

Cyrexa is a digital bank that enforces legitimate transactions and provides funds for settlements in Europe. Anyone can easily open an account and apply for a debit card, which can be used at any stores that accept VISA and Mastercard.

Cyrex Inc.(Payment Service Provider)

Cyrex Inc. is a Payment Service Provider that offers global credit card transaction services and is integrated to the GURUFIN network.

GURUFIN WALLET & SCAN

GURUFIN WALLET & SCAN

GURUFIN Wallet

The GURUFIN Wallet manages digital assets and offers a variety of functions. The multi-sig security technology allows users to safely access their accounts and assets on any mobile device, as long as users can provide the necessary login information.

GURUFIN Scan

GURUFIN Scan allows users to check transactions and details issued through the GURUFIN network. All block, transaction, such as transmission, delegation, compensation, and more, and verifier node information is provided and can be searched with block number, transaction ID or user wallet address.
